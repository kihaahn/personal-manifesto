Self-Knowledge

I know who I am and what matters.

I have unbending sense of identity.

With this clarity I cut through the noise.

Self-Realization

I seek within.

Love, fulfillment, happiness, strength, motivation, pride, growth, wisdom…

All these values already exist within me.

All I need to do is to recognize, tap in, and cultivate them.

I know that external support can assist, lubricate, and accentuate my inner values.

But I firmly understand that they cannot be the source.

I know that true greatness is not somewhere out there, something I need to acquire.

I do not seek outside to find them.

I seek within.

Sincerity

I am sincere.

I know how precious life is, and I don’t waste it with an ounce insincerity.

When I say something, I mean it.

When I show up, I show up as who I am.

The way I show respect is through my eyes — I look eye to eye.

Confidence

I am unappologetically me.

Life is too precious to worry about what other people think of me.

I get to the the point.

I speak my truth.

Intentionality

I begin with the end in mind.

I look ahead and act with intension.

I don’t waste my moves.

Intuition

I know how to tune into my intuition.

I have to courage trust my instict.

Responsibility

I am the designer of my life.

My life is my responsibility.

I don’t point fingers.

I take initiatives.

Self-Regulation

I am a master of my own thoughts, emotions, and actions.

I can manage my urges.

I take charge of my own state of mind.

Resilience

I know how to take shit.

I don’t let discomfort get in the way of achieving what I want.

I have befriended discomfort instead of resisting it.

Grit

I’m a fighter.

I fight till the end.

I don’t give up.

Flexibility

I am not stubborn.

I am not brittle.

I know how to bend.

I embrace change.

Balance

I know that anything extreme can be detrimental.

I balance my perspectives.

I can recognize when I sway too far and can recenter myself.

Acceptance

I accept the reality and make peace with it even as I’m trying to change it.

I don’t fight with nature.

If I don’t like something it, I give it a hug.

A radical disarmament.

Patience

I don’t let impatience rob off the joy of my life.

Life is too precious.

I enjoy the journey, even the painful one.

It’s all my life, every single one of them.

I do not rush.

Excellence

I know my standards.

And I uphold them — especially when it’s easier not to.

I don’t compromise core principles.

Good enough is not enough for me.

I do not accept mediocrety.

This is my life.

I do my utmost.

Selectivity

I devote my entire self — but on few things only.

I focus intensely.

I don’t get distracted by the shiny new things.

I put first things first.

I give it my full attention.

Preparedness

I do my homework.

I show up ready.

I put in the work.

Intensity

I got a fire in my belly.

I approach everything with heartfelt intensity.

Everything I do is personal to me.

I care with my soul.

Optimism

I have a vision.

I imagine the future that doesn’t exist yet.

I am hopeful in the face of difficulties.

I never doubt in my capacity to make a postive difference.

Curiosity

I am insatiably curious.

I am always headed towards the unknown.

I embrace confusion.

Humility

I am not perfect.

I can make mistake.

I seek honest feedback without defending myself.

I put my ego aside and always speak at an eye level.

Beginner’s Mindset

I don’t know everything.

I am here to learn.

I question things that are too obvious or certain.

I regularly rethink my beliefs.

I am untethered by the past.

Non-Judgment

I don’t plunge into first-glance judgement — both towards others and myself.

I seek to understand before forming my opinion.

I always practice empathy and deep listening.

Honesty

I don’t shy away from facing the truth.

I have the courage to have the uncomfortable conversation.

I am honest with myself and others.

Awareness

I am present.

I am mindful of my own mind.

I am observant and attentive to the moment and surroundings.

Wonder

I have a deep sense of wonder.

I engage with life by becoming genuinely interested.

I am always in awe of the magic of life.

Warmth

I always smile.

I always reach for hug — physically and metaphorically.

I emanate warmth in my voice.

Compassion

I see our common humanity.

I feel the struggles of others.

I rejoice in the success of others.

I share the ride together.

Forgiveness

I always forgive myself immediately and fully.

That is to say — I don’t hold grudge.

I take accountability but I don’t blame myself.

I accept my humanness.

And I extend this forgiveness to everyone else.

Ease

I am at ease.

I am fully engaged but I don’t tense up.

I dance in rhythm.

I glide through the waves without crashing it.

Generosity

I am generous of my spirit, attention, time, and resources.

I give without expectation of return.

I think in abundance.

Appreciation

I notice the small details.

I am always grateful.

I always make sure to express my appreciation in ways that people can feel it.

Intimacy

I know how to be intimate.

I can be vulnerable.

I can be close with others.

Playfulness

I am always playful.

I have a sense of humor.

I don’t take myself too seriously.

I have fun.
